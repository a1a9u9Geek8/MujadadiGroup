Agriculture Services

Comprehensive agricultural solutions from farm to table

Crop Farming

Cultivation of grains (maize, sorghum, millet, rice) and cash crops (cotton, soy, cassava) using modern sustainable farming techniques.

What we do: We operate large-scale farms across Nigeria and West Africa, utilizing precision agriculture, drought-resistant seeds, and sustainable irrigation systems. Our crop rotation programs maintain soil health while maximizing yields for both local consumption and export markets.

Poultry Division

End-to-end chicken production pipeline covering layers, broilers, and hatchery operations.

What we do: Our modern poultry facilities house over 100,000 birds with automated feeding, climate control, and health monitoring systems. We supply fresh eggs daily to major retailers and process thousands of broilers weekly for both local markets and restaurants.
  • Layers: Table-egg production & distribution
  • Broilers: Meat production (live & frozen)
  • Hatchery: Breeding, hatching, and chick supply

Dairy Production

Cattle farms producing raw milk, yogurt, butter, and other dairy products with the highest quality standards.

What we do: Our dairy farms maintain herds of Holstein and local breeds, producing over 5,000 liters of milk daily. We operate modern processing facilities that pasteurize, package, and distribute fresh dairy products to supermarkets, schools, and hospitals across the region.

Fishery & Aquaculture

Tilapia and catfish farms, feed mills, and processing facilities for local and export markets.

What we do: Our aquaculture facilities span 50+ ponds with recirculating water systems and automated feeding. We produce 200+ tons of fish annually, operating our own feed mills and processing plants that clean, fillet, and freeze fish for wholesale and retail distribution.
  • Tilapia farming
  • Catfish production
  • Fish processing & packaging

Goat & Livestock Farming

Comprehensive meat and breeding programs for goats, sheep, and cattle with focus on genetic improvement.

What we do: We maintain breeding stock of improved breeds including Boer goats, Dorper sheep, and Brahman cattle. Our veterinary team ensures optimal health while our breeding programs focus on disease resistance, faster growth rates, and higher meat quality for premium markets.

Fertilizer & Agro-Chemicals

Production and distribution of organic & inorganic fertilizers, pesticides, and soil conditioners.

What we do: Our manufacturing facility produces NPK fertilizers, organic compost from farm waste, and bio-pesticides. We operate a network of agro-dealers across rural areas, providing farmers with quality inputs, soil testing services, and technical advice to maximize crop yields sustainably.
  • Organic fertilizers
  • Inorganic fertilizers
  • Soil conditioners

Animal Feed Production

Feed mill plants producing high-quality poultry, fish, and livestock feed blends with optimal nutrition.

What we do: Our feed mills process maize, soybean, and other grains into nutritionally balanced feeds. We formulate specific recipes for different animal ages and production stages, ensuring optimal growth rates and health. Our quality control lab tests every batch for protein content and safety.

Agro-Machinery & Equipment

Supply and leasing of tractors, planters, harvesters, irrigation systems and modern farming equipment.

What we do: We import and distribute John Deere, Massey Ferguson, and other quality brands. Our leasing program helps smallholder farmers access expensive machinery with flexible payment plans. We also provide maintenance services, spare parts, and operator training to ensure maximum equipment efficiency.
  • Tractors & planters
  • Harvesters
  • Irrigation systems

Food Processing & Packaging

Value-addition through grain milling, vegetable oil extraction, fruit juice packaging and food preservation.

What we do: Our processing facilities transform raw agricultural products into consumer-ready goods. We mill grains into flour, extract oil from groundnuts and soybeans, package fresh fruit juices, and use solar drying and cold storage to preserve foods. All products meet international food safety standards.
